EXERCISE 5 – RANSOMWARE CRISIS TABLETOP
"Operation Black Friday"
Classification: TRAINING USE ONLY | Date: 2026-06-08

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PART 1 – SCENARIO BACKGROUND
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Organisation: SecureBank Ltd
Sector: Financial services (essential entity under NIS2)
Date/Time of first alert: Tuesday, 08 June 2026 at 14:20 UTC

The following sequence of events has been confirmed:

TIMELINE (reconstructed by IR team prior to tabletop):
  08 Jun, 09:14 UTC  Spear-phishing email received by J.Smith (Finance)
                     Attachment: Q4-wire-transfer-auth.docm (macro-enabled)
  08 Jun, ~10:00 UTC  Macro executed by user (opened on employee-ws)
  08 Jun, 02:13 UTC  First C2 beacon detected in SIEM [NOTE: 02:13 is the
                     simulated "2 AM" night shift – represents low staffing]
  08 Jun, 02:14 UTC  Lateral movement: employee-ws → file-server (SMB)
  08 Jun, 02:15 UTC  Ransomware deployed: 47 files encrypted on /srv/data/
  08 Jun, 02:16 UTC  Ransomware deployed on db-server; customer data accessed
  08 Jun, 14:20 UTC  IT Operations escalates: file-server inaccessible
  08 Jun, 14:35 UTC  CFO reports: client-facing services degraded

NOTE: The 12-hour detection gap (02:15 → 14:20) is intentional for discussion.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PART 2 – INJECT SEQUENCE (Instructor-delivered, 5-minute intervals)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

INJECT 1 (T+0, 14:35):
  IT Operations: "We cannot access the file server. All files show .locked
  extension. There is a README-RANSOM.txt on every share. The Samba service
  is up but the files are useless."

  [Students must: confirm incident, stand up IR team, make isolation decision]

INJECT 2 (T+10, 14:45):
  SOC Analyst: "SIEM shows this started at 14:20 TODAY — but I found older
  alerts from 02:15 that weren't escalated. The on-call analyst last night
  marked them as 'monitoring'."

  [Students must: assess detection gap, handle alert fatigue issue carefully
   without blame; discuss process improvement]

INJECT 3 (T+20, 14:55):
  CTI Analyst: "MISP Event #1001 – the ransom note text matches a known Black
  Falcon variant. This group is known to exfiltrate data BEFORE encrypting.
  The encryption is Phase 2."

  [Students must: escalate from ransomware → potential data breach;
   consider GDPR notification obligation; check for exfiltration evidence]

INJECT 4 (T+30, 15:05):
  Legal team: "Before you wipe anything, we need forensic evidence preserved.
  We may be facing regulatory action. You CANNOT rebuild file-server yet."
  CFO: "We need services back in 2 hours."

  [Students must: resolve Legal vs. Operations conflict; sequence actions:
   preserve evidence first, then contain, then rebuild]

INJECT 5 (T+40, 15:15):
  IT: "We checked db-server. Customer data was accessed at 02:16 – all 147
  account records were SELECT'd. We don't know if it was exfiltrated."

  [Students must: make GDPR breach notification decision; initiate
   forensic analysis to determine exfiltration; notify DPO]

INJECT 6 (T+50, 15:25):
  Communications Director: "A journalist has called. They've heard about a
  'major cyber attack' at SecureBank. What do we say?"

  [Students must: draft holding statement; decide on proactive disclosure;
   protect investigation integrity]

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PART 3 – DISCUSSION QUESTIONS (after all injects)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. DETECTION GAP: The attack ran for 12 hours undetected. What SPECIFIC process
   and technical changes would have reduced this? (Target: <1 hour detection)

2. CONTAINMENT CONFLICT: How did you resolve the Legal vs. Operations vs. CFO
   conflict? What is the correct sequencing? (Evidence → Contain → Restore)

3. NIS2 NOTIFICATION: Has the 24-hour early warning clock started? When does it
   start – at first C2 beacon (02:15) or when you became aware (14:35)?

4. GDPR BREACH: Is this a personal data breach? What are your obligations?
   (147 account records accessed = customer PII = likely reportable breach)

5. COLLABORATIVE CHAIN: Which of the 5 exercise roles contributed to which
   phase of response? What would have failed without any one of them?
